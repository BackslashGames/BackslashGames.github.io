Arrow Standoff

Arrow Standoff is a multiplayer minecraft minigame for servers. You are randomly put on a team and brought to the arena. The aim is to shoot other players and kill them. The teams each have seperate platforms, and can't shoot each other.

It has a money system using scoreboards. You gain money by getting kills and it can be spent in the ingame shop in the lobby. The shop isn't yet finished as we are remaking it in minecraft 1.9 with the new command blocks system.
